What we learned

Apps first

  • SBB Mobile for trains — check Supersaver fares
  • MeteoSwiss + webcams before any summit day
  • Jungfrau app for live lift status & tickets
  • Google Maps offline — valleys have dead zones

Driving

  • Vignette included on the rental
  • Speed cameras everywhere — set cruise control
  • Fuel up in villages, ~15% cheaper than highway
  • Park edge-of-town and walk into old centers

Rhythm of the days

  • Start early — the mountains are quietest and clearest then
  • Long, unhurried afternoons back at the chalet
  • A carrier beats the stroller on the trails
  • Layers always — it's winter at the summits

Money

  • Always pay in CHF — decline USD conversion
  • CSR / Venture X — no foreign transaction fees
  • Carry CHF 100–200 for huts & meters
  • Coop / Migros close Sundays — stock up Saturday

Golf day

  • Golf Grindelwald — Aspistrasse 7, 9 holes
  • No handicap required; play permit on arrival
  • Rent clubs on-site, ~CHF 40 green fee
  • Call ahead to reserve a morning tee time
