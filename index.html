Essential Tips

What we learned from planning this trip

Transportation

  • Swiss Travel Pass: 8 days for 4 adults = $2,080
  • Toddler rides free with Family Card
  • 50% off Jungfraujoch, free Mt Rigi
  • Includes boats and mountain railways

With a Toddler

  • Build in 2-3 hour afternoon naps daily
  • Early starts (7-8 AM) before crowds
  • Bring baby carrier for non-stroller terrain
  • Pack warm layers - cold at altitude!
  • Flexible schedule for meltdowns

Packing Essentials

  • Winter coat for Jungfraujoch
  • Lightweight stroller + baby carrier
  • Rain gear and layers
  • Portable high chair seat
  • Emergency snacks and sippy cups

Golf Day

  • Interlaken-Unterseen Golf Club (9 holes)
  • Club rental: CHF 30-50 (~$35-55)
  • Book 1-2 weeks in advance
  • No need to pack clubs - rent everything
  • Mountain views while you play!

Amex FHR Benefits

  • $100 property credit per stay
  • Daily breakfast for 2 included
  • Room upgrade when available
  • 4 PM late checkout guaranteed
  • Average value: $550 per stay

Budget Estimate

  • Hotels: $8,000 - $12,000
  • Swiss Travel Pass: $2,080
  • Mountain excursions: $400-600
  • Meals: $2,000 - $3,000
  • Total: $13,000 - $18,500
